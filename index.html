Our Vision

CERL is dedicated to provide long-term environmental solutions to our clients that cover specific environmental goals to enhance the quality of business and to help them in their operational activities in a sustainable manner.

CERL aims to maintain quality standard in its monitoring and technical services under international guidelines and prolong friendly relationship with the customers .

Our Objectives

  • Provide the Quality Monitoring to our Client as per international Standard.
  • Support to the client to meet their audits goals..
  • Help our customers in the sound implementation of Sindh Environmental Quality Standards (SEQs) of SEP Act Regulation 2014.
  • Provide awareness to the work force & general people about the environmental & safety hazards.

Our Services

"State-of-the-art environmental testing and fuel analysis lab ensuring quality, compliance, and client satisfaction."

Ambient Air Quality

CERL has well-equipped ambient air quality monitoring stations to measure quality parameters for air like carbon monoxide, oxides of nitrogen and sulfur, and particles of different sizes. We are offering indoor and ambient air quality monitoring services for pollutants as per provincial and international guidelines. We have the capability for 24-hour air quality monitoring. .

Exhaust Emission

CERL offers exhaust monitor monitoring of combustibles and motilities for quality parameters like smoke, carbon monoxide, oxides of nitrogen and sulfur, and particulate matter. We are offering indoor and ambient air quality monitoring services for pollutants as per provincial and international guidelines.

Water Quality Analysis

CERL has established a wet lab that is fully equipped with the latest water testing equipment and has a capacity of 32 SEQs for quality parameters for both wastewater and drinking water. All the testing would be done under the standard operating procedures of ASTM, APHA, and USEPA. .

Noise & LUX Monitoring

CERL offers Nosie and Lux monitoring through its highly precise and calibrated Noise and Lux meter. Monitoring would be made as per SEQs (Sindh Environmental Quality Standard) of the SEP Act 2014 and OSHAS guidelines. These parameters have an impact on human health .

Fuel Testing Facility

CERL has established a solid fuel testing facility equipped with the latest equipment, like a bomb colorimeter, for the proximate analysis of coal, coke, bagasse, and other solid fuels.

Sludge & Soil Testing

CERL has capacity for sludge and soil analysis with different analytical parameters like heavy metals, phosphate, nitrate, calcium, etc. All the analysis would be done under the ASTM/APHA operating procedures.
